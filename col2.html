About D.Y. Patil College of Engineering and Technology

D.Y. Patil College of Engineering and Technology is a prestigious institution located in Kolhapur, dedicated to excellence in education and innovation in engineering and technology. Established with a vision to nurture future leaders, the college offers a variety of programs in engineering and applied sciences.

  • Strong industry collaborations and placement records
  • Focus on research, innovation, and entrepreneurship
